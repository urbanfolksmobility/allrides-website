Questions?

Commonly Asked

Where is allrides available right now?
Our buses are live across the Trivandrum and Kollam districts of Kerala. We're expanding steadily, so more routes and regions are on the way.
How do I buy a bus ticket with the app?
Download the app, scan the QR code inside the bus, choose your stops, and pay. Your paperless E-ticket is ready on your phone in seconds.
Do I still need a paper ticket?
No. Your E-ticket on the phone is enough — just share your Ticket Code with the conductor if asked. Prefer paper? You can still request one from the conductor.
When are bike rentals coming?
Soon! Bike rental bookings are opening up shortly, so you'll be able to book a ride and explore — all from the same allrides app.
Who is allrides built for?
For everyone on the move — daily commuters and travellers alike — who just want to get around hassle-free, without cash, queues, or guesswork.
The app is in beta — what does that mean for me?
We're in our early beta builds, so you may run into a few hiccups. Your feedback genuinely shapes what we build next — your support keeps us improving every ride.
Is making a payment safe and simple?
Yes. Pay securely from your phone — no cash, no fumbling for change. Scan, pay, and ride. That's it.
