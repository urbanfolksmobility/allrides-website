Commonly Asked

Why should I use Allrides instead of booking directly?
Because you shouldn’t have to check 10 websites, make multiple calls, or visit offices just to compare prices and availability. Allrides lists trusted rentals in one place, so you can book in minutes.
Are the bikes and rentals trustworthy?
Yes. We work only with verified local rental partners. Clear pricing, reliable bikes, and no last-minute surprises.
Do I need to visit a rental office after booking?
No unnecessary visits. Book online, get the details, and pick up your bike smoothly.
How is Allrides different from Google Maps?
Google Maps helps you reach places. Allrides helps you discover them. We focus on routes, spots, and places with real local vibe—the kind you’d miss if you only followed navigation.
I’m new to Varkala. Will this still work for me?
That’s exactly who it’s built for. Whether a place is next to you or 5 km away, Allrides helps you find it without overthinking.
Can I choose bikes based on my plan?
Absolutely. Whether it’s a short ride, a full-day explore, or multiple days—pick what fits your trip.
Is booking complicated?
Not at all. Choose a bike, check availability, book, and ride. That’s it.
