How it works

Get your ticket in 5 simple steps

Everything you need to ride this bus with a paperless online ticket.

1

Download the allrides app

Grab it from the Play Store using the button above, or by scanning the QR code in this bus.

2

Scan the QR code

You'll find the QR code at the front of the bus and on the back of the seats.

3

Choose your stops

The stops for this bus appear automatically — just pick where you're getting on and off.

4

Pay & get your ticket

Complete the payment and your online ticket is instantly ready on your phone.

5

Show your ticket

For checking, tell the conductor your Ticket Code (TICKET ID) shown on your ticket.

Need a paper ticket? You can ask the conductor for one — just keep your Ticket Code ready, as it is mandatory.
